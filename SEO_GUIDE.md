## üöÄ Steps to Get Indexed by Search Engines

### 1. Google Search Console (Most Important)

**Setup Steps:**
1. Go to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console)
2. Click "Add Property"
3. Enter your URL: `https://indranil2020.github.io`
4. Verify ownership (GitHub Pages makes this easy):
   - Choose "HTML tag" method
   - Copy the meta tag provided
   - Add it to the `<head>` section of your index.html
   - Click "Verify"

**After Verification:**
1. Submit your sitemap:
   - Go to "Sitemaps" in the left menu
   - Enter: `sitemap.xml`
   - Click "Submit"
2. Request indexing:
   - Go to "URL Inspection"
   - Enter: `https://indranil2020.github.io`
   - Click "Request Indexing"

**Expected Timeline:**
- Initial crawl: 24-48 hours
- Full indexing: 1-2 weeks
- Ranking: 2-4 weeks (improves over time)
