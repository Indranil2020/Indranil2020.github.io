# NanoNet

## Overview
**NanoNet** is a tight-binding package designed for electronic structure calculations of nanostructures including nanowires, quantum dots, and heterostructures.

## Official Resources
- **GitHub**: Search for NanoNet tight-binding

## Capabilities
- **Nanostructures**: Nanowires, quantum dots
- **Heterostructures**: Interface modeling
- **Band Structure**: Electronic bands
- **Transport**: Quantum transport

## Key Features
- Nanostructure focus
- Heterostructure support
- Transport calculations
- Flexible geometry

## Applications
- Semiconductor nanowires
- Quantum dots
- Core-shell structures
- Device modeling
