# elphem

## Overview
**elphem** is a tool for electron-phonon calculations using the empty lattice (free electron) model, providing a simplified approach to electron-phonon coupling.

## Official Resources
- **GitHub**: Search for elphem electron-phonon

## Capabilities
- **Empty Lattice Model**: Free electron approximation
- **Electron-Phonon**: Coupling calculations
- **Band Structure**: Simple band models
- **Transport**: Basic transport properties

## Key Features
- Simplified model
- Fast calculations
- Educational tool
- Electron-phonon focus

## Applications
- Simple metal modeling
- Electron-phonon studies
- Teaching materials
- Quick estimates

## Limitations & Known Constraints
- **Simplified model**: Empty lattice approximation
- **Accuracy**: Not suitable for detailed quantitative predictions
- **Educational focus**: Better for understanding than production

## Comparison with Other Tools
- **vs EPW**: elphem simplified, EPW full DFT-based
- **vs Wannier-based tools**: Different level of theory
- **Unique strength**: Simple model for electron-phonon understanding
