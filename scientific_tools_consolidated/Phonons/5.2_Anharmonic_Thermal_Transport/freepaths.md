## Overview
freepaths is a Monte Carlo simulator of phonon transport in nanostructures. It simulates trajectories of phonons in 3D models with various geometries including holes, pillars, and complex boundaries, outputting thermal conductivity, heat fluxes, and temperature maps.

**Scientific domain**: Phonon Monte Carlo, nanostructure thermal transport  
**Target user community**: Researchers studying thermal transport in nanostructured materials

## Theoretical Methods
- Monte Carlo ray tracing
- Phonon transport simulation
- Boundary scattering
- Specular and diffuse reflection
- Phonon dispersion models
- Statistical sampling
