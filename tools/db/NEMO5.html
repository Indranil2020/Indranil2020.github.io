Overview

**NEMO5** (NanoElectronics MOdeling Tools 5) is a state-of-the-art **multiscale simulation framework** for nanoelectronics. It represents the culmination of decades of development (NEMO-1D, NEMO-3D, OMEN) and integrates atomistic tight-binding models with continuum approximations, strain engineering, phonon transport, and optical properties.
