Overview

ORCA is a modern, general-purpose quantum chemistry program package featuring extensive capabilities in molecular electronic structure calculations. Developed by Frank Neese and coworkers at the Max Planck Institute für Kohlenforschung, ORCA is known for its user-friendly input, comprehensive methods (from semi-empirical to high-level coupled cluster), excellent spectroscopic property calculations, and efficient parallelization. Version 6.0 (released July 2024) represents a near-complete rewrite
