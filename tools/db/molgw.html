Overview

molgw is an open-source quantum chemistry code implementing many-body perturbation theory (GW approximation and Bethe-Salpeter equation) for molecules and clusters using Gaussian basis sets. Developed by Fabien Bruneval (CEA, France), molgw focuses on efficient GW calculations for finite systems with emphasis on accurate ionization potentials, electron affinities, and optical excitations.
