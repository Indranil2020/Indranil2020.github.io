Overview

**almaBTE** is a high-performance C++ software package designed for calculating the **lattice thermal conductivity** and other phonon transport properties of materials from first principles. It solves the **Peierls-Boltzmann Transport Equation (BTE)** for phonons, moving beyond the relaxation time approximation (RTA) to capture full scattering processes, including three-phonon interactions.
