Overview

**EDRIXS** (Exact Diagonalization for Resonant Inelastic X-ray Scattering) is an open-source toolkit designed for simulating X-ray Absorption Spectroscopy (XAS), Resonant Inelastic X-ray Scattering (RIXS), and Resonant Magnetic X-ray Scattering (RMXS). Built on exact diagonalization (ED) of model Hamiltonians, it is particularly suited for studying strongly correlated materials where local interactions play a critical role.
