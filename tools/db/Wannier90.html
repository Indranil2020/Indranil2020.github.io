Overview

Wannier90 is the community standard code for computing maximally-localized Wannier functions (MLWFs) from electronic structure calculations. Developed by an international collaboration, Wannier90 transforms delocalized Bloch states from DFT calculations into localized Wannier representations, enabling tight-binding model construction, topological analysis, transport calculations, and many other applications.
