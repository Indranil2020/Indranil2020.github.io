Overview

ACES III is the modern successor to ACES II, representing a complete redesign of the ACES quantum chemistry package for parallel computing environments. Developed at the University of Florida's Quantum Theory Project, ACES III implements coupled cluster methods using the Super Instruction Assembly Language (SIAL) for automatic parallelization.
