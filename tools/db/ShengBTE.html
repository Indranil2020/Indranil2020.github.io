Overview

**ShengBTE** is a widely used software package for solving the **Phonon Boltzmann Transport Equation (BTE)** to calculate the lattice thermal conductivity of crystalline materials. It operates on a fully *ab initio* basis, taking second-order (harmonic) and third-order (anharmonic) interatomic force constants (IFCs) from density functional theory (DFT) calculations as input.
