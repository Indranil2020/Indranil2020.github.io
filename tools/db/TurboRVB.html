Overview

TurboRVB is a high-performance quantum Monte Carlo package developed at SISSA (International School for Advanced Studies, Trieste) with emphasis on strongly correlated systems, superconductors, and resonating valence bond (RVB) physics. The code implements advanced trial wavefunctions including Jastrow-geminal-Slater forms, AGP (antisymmetrized geminal power), and pairing functions optimized for studying correlation effects, superconductivity, and quantum phase transitions.
