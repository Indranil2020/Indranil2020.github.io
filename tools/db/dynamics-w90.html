Overview

**dynamics-w90** is a sophisticated Fortran package designed to simulate **non-equilibrium electron dynamics** in solids using realistic tight-binding Hamiltonians derived from **Wannier90**. It focuses on light-matter interactions, enabling the study of ultrafast phenomena such as high-harmonic generation (HHG), time-resolved photoemission (tr-ARPES), and transient band structure engineering.
