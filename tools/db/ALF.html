Overview

**ALF** (Algorithms for Lattice Fermions) is a high-performance, open-source software package designed for **Quantum Monte Carlo (QMC)** simulations of strongly correlated fermion systems. It specifically implements the **Auxiliary-Field QMC (AFQMC)** method (also known as Determinantal QMC) to solve a wide class of lattice models at finite temperature or in the ground state (projective).
