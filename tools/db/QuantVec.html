Overview

QuantVec is the successor to AIMPAC2 and is being developed as an open suite of QTAIM and Quantum Chemical Topology tools. The repository describes Python-based, pip-installable tools, example datasets, utilities, and a `topviz` molecular-graph visualization GUI for QTAIM-related analysis workflows.
