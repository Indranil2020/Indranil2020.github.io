Overview

**HopTB.jl** is a Julia package designed for constructing and analyzing tight-binding Hamiltonians, with a unique focus on **non-orthogonal bases**. It serves as a bridge between first-principles Density Functional Theory (DFT) codes and model physics, allowing users to import Hamiltonians from **Wannier90**, **OpenMX**, and **FHI-aims**.
